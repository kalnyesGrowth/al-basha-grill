Our Story

A Taste of Palestine, Right Here in Stafford

Founded in 2022 by Firas Abdulaziz, Al Basha Grill was born from a deep passion for sharing the rich culinary traditions of Palestine with the Stafford community.

Every dish we serve is crafted with the same care and recipes that have been passed down through generations. From our slow-roasted shawarma to our perfectly crispy falafel, each plate tells the story of our heritage.

We believe great food brings people together. That's why we focus on quality ingredients, authentic preparation, and the kind of warm hospitality you'd find in a Palestinian home.

- Firas Abdulaziz, Founder
